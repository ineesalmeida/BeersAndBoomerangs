Description

A Chaotic Survival/Cooking VR game where kangaroos are stealing your beer! Multi-task between cooking for your mates and assaulting Australian fauna. Master your fully physics-based boomerangs and defend your BBQ

Main mechanics

Boomerang throwing (duh)
Cooking and serving

Features

Amazing Physics-based boomerangs
Campaign mode with 15 levels
Boss Fights!
Endless arcade mode
Lovely Annoying Australian Fauna
Beer skins
"Boomerang" skins
Vegetarian BBQ alternatives
Non-Alcoholic drink alternatives

About us

We are a couple of Portuguese biomedical engineers, that one day decided to build a video game. In January of 2021 Diogo quit his job and decided to work full-time on Beers and Boomerangs while Inês worked part-time. In March 2022, Inês also quit her job so we finally became a proper team of two! We are both generalists and tried to do most of the code, music and art by ourselves. If you want us in your blog/podcast/talks reach out to diogo@beersandboomerangs.com
